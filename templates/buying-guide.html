Eyewear Buying Guide

Confused about which glasses to buy? Here's everything you need to know before picking your perfect pair.

1. Understand Your Face Shape

Choose frames that complement your face shape. For example:

  • Round Face: Rectangular or square frames
  • Square Face: Round or oval frames
  • Oval Face: Almost any shape suits you

2. Choose the Right Lens

Your lens depends on your usage and prescription:

  • Single Vision – for reading or distance
  • Progressive – for multi-distance use (no bifocal lines)
  • Blue Light Blocking – for screen protection
  • Photochromatic – auto-darken in sunlight

3. Pick Your Style

Eyeglasses are not just vision aids, they’re fashion statements. Choose from classic, bold, rimless, wayfarers, aviators, and more!

4. Consider Frame Material

Each material has its own pros:

  • Acetate – Lightweight and colorful
  • Metal – Durable and professional
  • TR90 – Flexible and sporty
