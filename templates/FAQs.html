Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our eyeglasses or sunglasses, select a frame, and click "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout and provide your shipping details to complete the order.

2. Do you offer a virtual try-on feature?

Yes! On the Try-On page, you can test how each frame looks on you using your webcam or an uploaded photo.

3. How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 5–7 business days depending on your location.

4. What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day hassle-free return policy on all non-customized products. Frames with prescription lenses are not eligible for return unless damaged or defective.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can email us at ghfyujkg@gmail.com for any help or inquiries.
